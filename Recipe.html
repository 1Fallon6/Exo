How to make a choclate mug cake

ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • half a tea spoon of butter, preferably melted in the microwave before mixing (careful, it melts really fast)
  • 2 big spoons of milk
  • melted choclate, nutella or the like, up to your prefrence
  • and a big spoon and a half of flour, or two, depends on how you will like it

The steps to make it:

  1. melt the butter
  2. put the mentioned amount of flour in the mug
  3. next is the egg
  4. pour the melted choclate on top
  5. then the milk
  6. mix
  7. pour the melted butter in
  8. mix again
